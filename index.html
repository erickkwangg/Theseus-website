Tensor Commits

Theseus introduces Tensor Commits, developed with collaborators at UT Austin and Georgia Tech, a truly groundbreaking inference validation mechanism enabling decentralized, trustless verification of AI computations. Tensor Commits builds on over a decade of research on vector commitments and generalizes it for Tensor operations. Tensor commits are able to prove inference with ~0.1% proof compute overhead and a less than 1MB proof size, crucially supporting all types of deep learning models and extendable to thousands of validators and inference providers.
